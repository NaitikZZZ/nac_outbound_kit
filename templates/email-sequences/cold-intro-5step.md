## Smartlead Email Steps (5 steps — auto-sent)

**Subject A:** `{{first_name}}, quick question about rewards at {{company_name}}`
**Subject B:** `Your users earn. What do they redeem?`

```html

<p>Quick question: if {{company_name}} runs any kind of points, cashback, referral, or engagement program — what does the redemption experience look like for your users?</p>

<p>Most companies we speak with have built a strong earn engine but struggle with the burn side — limited reward options, single-geography catalogues, or high fulfilment overhead.</p>

<p>We power the rewards infrastructure for companies like Viwell, HDB Financial, and Curefit — giving their users instant access to 10,000+ reward options (gift cards, experiences, merchandise, charity, mobile top-ups) across 100+ countries. All through a single API integration that goes live in 2–3 weeks.</p>

<p>If this is relevant to what {{company_name}} is building, happy to discuss. 15 min, no commitment.</p>

<p>{{sender_first_name}}<br/>Xoxoday | Plum</p>
```

## Manual Touches (ABM/SDR team — not in Smartlead)

### D1 — LinkedIn Profile Visit (same day as Email 1)
**Owner:** ABM
**Action:** Visit the prospect's LinkedIn profile. Let the notification land alongside the email. No connect request yet.

### D2 — LinkedIn Like/Comment
**Owner:** ABM
**Action:** Find a recent post from the prospect. Like it. Leave a genuine 1–2 sentence comment if there's something substantive. No pitch.

### D4 — LinkedIn Connection Request (no note)
**Owner:** ABM / HeyReach
**Action:** Clean connection request, no message attached. Acceptance rates are higher without a pitch.

### D5 — SDR Call 1
**Owner:** SDR
**Script:** "Hi {{first_name}}, this is [SDR Name] from Xoxoday. I sent you an email about Plum — we build the rewards infrastructure that companies plug into when they need a global redemption catalogue. Wanted to check if that's something {{company_name}} is exploring. [If yes:] Great — I can get you sandbox access or set up a 15-min walkthrough. [If no:] Totally understand — if it ever comes up, we're easy to reach."
**Voicemail:** "Hey {{first_name}}, [SDR Name] from Xoxoday. Sent you a note about Plum's rewards API — just putting a voice to the name. Would love 15 minutes if the timing works. I'll follow up by email."

### D7 — LinkedIn DM (if connected) / InMail (if not)
**Owner:** ABM
**Message:**
> Hey {{first_name}} — sent a couple of emails about Plum's rewards API. Not sure if they landed, so thought I'd try here. Quick version: we power the redemption layer for companies across 100+ countries — gift cards, experiences, perks, and more. One API, 2–3 weeks to go live. If {{company_name}} is exploring anything like this, happy to share sandbox access. If not, no worries at all!

### D8 — SDR Call 2
**Owner:** SDR
**Action:** Second dial. Voicemail if no pickup: "Hey {{first_name}}, [SDR Name] again. Reached out a few times — just one last try by phone. If rewards infrastructure comes on the roadmap for {{company_name}}, I'd love to help. All the best."

### D10 — LinkedIn DM 2 / Value Add
**Owner:** ABM
