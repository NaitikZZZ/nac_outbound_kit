## Manual Touches (ABM/SDR team — not in Smartlead)

### D1 — LinkedIn Profile Visit (same day as Email 1)
**Owner:** ABM
**Action:** Visit the prospect's LinkedIn profile via Dux-Soup or manually. Don't connect yet — just let the "viewed your profile" notification land alongside the email.

### D2 — LinkedIn Like/Comment
**Owner:** ABM
**Action:** Find a recent post or activity from the prospect. Like it. If there's something substantive, leave a short comment (1–2 sentences, relevant to their industry — not a pitch).

### D4 — LinkedIn Connection Request (no note)
**Owner:** ABM / HeyReach
**Action:** Send a clean connection request with NO note. Acceptance rates are higher without a pitch in the request. If they accept, D7 DM becomes available.

### D5 — SDR Call 1
**Owner:** SDR
**Script:** "Hi {{first_name}}, this is [SDR Name] from Xoxoday. I'm following up on an email I sent about Plum — you explored a rewards integration with us a while back and I wanted to check if that's still on your radar. [If interested:] Great, I can set up a 15-min sandbox walkthrough with our solutions team. [If not:] Totally understand — I'll make a note. If it comes back up, we're easy to reach."
**Voicemail:** "Hey {{first_name}}, [SDR Name] from Xoxoday. Sent you an email about Plum's rewards API — just wanted to put a voice to the name. If the timing works, I'd love 15 minutes. I'll follow up by email."

### D7 — LinkedIn DM (if connected) / InMail (if not)
**Owner:** ABM
**Message:**
> Hey {{first_name}} — I've sent a couple of emails about Plum's rewards API. Not sure if they landed. Quick version: we power the redemption layer for apps like Viwell and HDB — 10,000+ rewards, 100+ countries, one API. If {{company_name}} is still exploring this, happy to share sandbox access. If not, no worries at all!

### D8 — SDR Call 2
**Owner:** SDR
**Action:** Second dial attempt. If no pickup, leave a voicemail referencing the LinkedIn + email touches. "Hey {{first_name}}, [SDR Name] again from Xoxoday. I've reached out a few times via email and LinkedIn — just wanted to try one more time by phone. If rewards infrastructure comes back on the roadmap for {{company_name}}, I'm here. All the best."

### D10 — LinkedIn DM 2 / Value Add
**Owner:** ABM
**Message:** Share a relevant content asset (case study PDF, ROI calculator link, or comparison infographic). Keep it short:
> {{first_name}} — thought this might be useful: [link to case study / comparison]. No pitch, just figured it's relevant to what {{company_name}} is building. Cheers!
