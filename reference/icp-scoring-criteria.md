# 🎯 Company Scoring & Tiering Criteria — Xoxoday

> **Xoxoday** is a global rewards, recognition, loyalty, and incentives platform.
> Product suite: **Empuls** (employee R&R + engagement), **Plum** (rewards/incentives/payouts API + 10M+ reward catalog across 175+ countries), **Compass** (sales commission automation), **Loyalife** (customer loyalty), **Loopr** (merchant offers).
>
> This rubric defaults to the **Empuls (HR / People Ops)** angle because it has the widest ICP. See the "Product Angle Switch" section at the bottom to retune the criteria for Plum (Marketing/RevOps buyers), Compass (Sales Ops/Finance buyers), or Loyalife (CX/Growth buyers).

## Scoring Framework (0-100)

| Factor | Max Points | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Industry Fit | 20 | BFSI, SaaS, IT Services, Retail/E-com, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Pharma |
| Company Size | 25 | Sweet spot 200–5,000 employees (enough HR budget + distributed workforce) |
| Geography | 15 | Multi-country operations = rewards catalog advantage |
| Buying Signals | 15 | Hiring HR leaders, running engagement surveys, recent funding |
| Tech Stack | 10 | Uses an HRIS/HCM; no existing R&R platform |
| Revenue | 10 | $20M–$500M ARR / revenue sweet spot |
| Compliance Need | 5 | Enterprises needing SOC 2 / ISO 27001 / GDPR — Xoxoday's moat |

## Factor Scoring Details

### Industry Fit (0-20 pts)
| Match Level | Points | Examples |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| Core match | 20 | IT services, SaaS, BFSI, BPO, large retail/e-com, pharma |
| Strong adjacency | 15 | Healthcare systems, telecom, manufacturing with knowledge workers |
| Moderate fit | 10 | Consulting, logistics, automotive, education groups |
| Weak fit | 5 | Small agencies, single-site retail, local services |
| No fit | 0 | Non-profits with <100 staff, sole proprietorships |

### Company Size (0-25 pts)
| Employees | Points | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1–50 | 3 | Below ROI threshold — low R&R spend, no dedicated HR |
| 51–200 | 12 | Entry fit — often bought by People Ops leaders |
| 201–1,000 | 25 | **Sweet spot** — dedicated HRBPs, budget, engagement mandate |
| 1,001–5,000 | 22 | Great — multi-country, multi-BU = big catalog leverage |
| 5,001–20,000 | 16 | Enterprise — longer cycle, Empuls scales but needs security review |
| 20,000+ | 10 | Enterprise — typically custom deal, long procurement |

### Geography (0-15 pts)
| Footprint | Points | Why it matters |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Operates in 5+ countries | 15 | Xoxoday's 175-country catalog is a killer differentiator |
| Operates in 2–4 countries | 12 | Multi-currency rewards still a real pain to self-build |
| Single-country, distributed cities | 8 | Still valuable vs gift cards/spreadsheets |
| Single-country, single-city | 5 | Fit, but lower wedge |
| Region Xoxoday doesn't support well | 0 | DQ |

### Buying Signals (0-15 pts)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Hiring VP/Director People, CHRO, Head of Rewards, Head of TA | 15 |
| Hiring Sales Ops Director / Incentive Compensation Manager (Compass angle) | 13 |
| Running an engagement survey (Glint, CultureAmp, Peakon mentions) | 12 |
| Recent funding round (Series B+) or IPO | 10 |
| Public mention of "employee engagement" / "recognition" initiative | 10 |
| Growing headcount 15%+ YoY | 8 |
| Expanding into new countries | 8 |
| No visible signals | 2 |

### Tech Stack (0-10 pts)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Uses Workday / SAP SuccessFactors / Oracle HCM / BambooHR / Darwinbox | 10 |
| Uses any modern HRIS (Keka, Zoho People, HiBob, Rippling) | 8 |
| Uses Salesforce + no visible R&R tool (Compass angle) | 8 |
| Only generic tools (G Suite, Slack, MS Teams) — greenfield | 6 |
| Already using a competitor (Vantage Circle, Achievers, O.C. Tanner, Workhuman) | 3 |
| Already on Xoxoday | 0 (DQ) |

### Revenue (0-10 pts)
| Revenue | Points |
|---------|--------|
| $50M – $500M | 10 |
| $20M – $50M | 8 |
| $500M – $2B | 7 |
| $5M – $20M | 5 |
| $2B+ | 6 (long cycle offset) |
| <$5M | 2 |

### Compliance Need (0-5 pts)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Regulated industry (BFSI, healthcare, pharma, defense) | 5 |
| Publicly traded / enterprise-grade procurement | 4 |
| Handles customer PII at scale | 3 |
| No explicit compliance posture | 1 |

## Tiering Thresholds

| Tier | Score | Strategy |
|------|-------|----------|
| 🥇 **Tier 1** | 75-100 | Highly personalized outreach. Direct value prop or case study (similar industry/size). CEO/CHRO-level sender. |
| 🥈 **Tier 2** | 50-74 | Semi-personalized, PAS framework. Cite catalog + multi-country reach as wedge. |
| 🥉 **Tier 3** | 25-49 | Template-based, social proof framework. Light touch, volume play. |
| ❌ **Disqualified** | <25 | Do not contact |

---

## Disqualification Criteria (Instant DQ)

- ❌ Already a Xoxoday / Empuls / Plum / Compass customer
- ❌ <50 employees and single-site (below ROI floor)
- ❌ Operates only in a country Xoxoday can't fulfill
- ❌ Locked into a multi-year contract with Achievers / Workhuman / Vantage Circle
- ❌ No discoverable buyer email contacts
- ❌ On DNC / blacklist

## Target Personas

Prioritize in this order when sourcing in Apollo / Sales Nav / Clay:

**Primary (Empuls angle — R&R / engagement):**
- CHRO, VP People, VP Human Resources
- Director / Head of Employee Experience
- Director / Head of Rewards & Recognition
- Director / Head of Total Rewards
- People Operations Lead

**Secondary (Plum angle — rewards API / marketing incentives):**
- VP / Head of Marketing
- Director of Growth / Demand Gen
- Head of Customer Marketing
- Head of Partnerships / Channel

**Secondary (Compass angle — sales commission automation):**
- VP Sales, Sales Operations Director
- Director of Revenue Operations
- Head of Incentive Compensation
- CFO (at orgs under 1,000 FTE)

**Secondary (Loyalife angle — customer loyalty):**
- VP / Head of CX, Chief Customer Officer
- Director of Loyalty / Retention

## Product Angle Switch

If you're running a campaign for a specific Xoxoday product, retune these weights:

| Product | Boost | Reduce |
|---------|-------|--------|
| **Empuls** (default above) | Company Size, Buying Signals (HR hires) | Sales-ops signals |
| **Plum** | Geography (multi-country), Industry (BFSI/retail/e-com), revenue | HR-only signals |
| **Compass** | Tech Stack (Salesforce/CRM), Sales team size, Revenue | HR hires |
| **Loyalife** | Consumer brands, retail/BFSI, D2C e-com, revenue | Company size (B2C focus) |
